# V10 Character Variants

New character sprites based on V9 style and format.

## Characters

### Knights (plate armor)
- **Dark Knight** - Black plate armor with spear (walk + thrust)
  - Human and dark-skinned variants
- **Paladin** - Gold/white plate armor (walk + thrust)
  - Human and dark-skinned variants
- **Crimson Knight** - Red/burgundy plate armor with spear (walk + thrust)

### Mages/Priests (robes)
- **Cleric** - White healing robes (walk + spellcast)
- **Ice Mage** - Cyan/ice blue robes (walk + spellcast)
- **Lightning Mage** - Yellow/gold robes (walk + spellcast)
- **Necromancer** - Dark purple robes with dark skin (walk + spellcast)
- **Lich** - Skeleton in purple robes (walk + spellcast)
- **Druid** - Skeleton in green robes (walk + spellcast)

### Warriors/Fighters
- **Assassin** - Dark leather armor, hood, dark skin (walk + slash)
- **Barbarian** - Minimal armor, pants + shoulders only (walk + slash)
- **Samurai** - Red/black chain armor (walk only)
- **Viking** - Brown/fur leather armor (walk + thrust)

### Monks
- **Monk** - Simple white shirt and pants with staff (walk + thrust)
- **Dark Monk** - Black robes with staff (walk + thrust)

## Format

Same as V9:
- 4 directions per sheet (down, left, right, up)
- 64x64 pixel frames
- Walk: 9 frames (576x256)
- Slash: 6 frames (384x256)
- Spellcast: 7 frames (448x256)
- Thrust: 8 frames (512x256)

Based on Liberated Pixel Cup (LPC) assets.
